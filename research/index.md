# Research Index — MareNostrum

## Foundation

This repository hosts [DHITL](../DHITL.md) — the **load-bearing architectural axiom** of the AI Safety anti-capture proposal. It also hosts the energy, economic, governance, and technical layer instantiations: Constellia (energy + food + digital sovereignty), the CXU (Compute Exergy Unit), the Charte du Soleil Méditerranéen, and the federated governance framework (ARCHITECTURE, GOVERNANCE, MODEL, CONTRACTS, PRICING). The method by which the proposal develops lives in [`barons-Mariani/research/second_method.md`](https://github.com/JeanHuguesRobert/barons-Mariani/blob/main/research/second_method.md).
